Why start this up?

I loved the charity initiatives in the community. Since I'm passionate about cryptocurrencies, I thought "Why not match meme charities with meme currencies" -Much simplicity.

Who has control of the wallet?

We’ve been working tirelessly for the past few days, and wanted to get this initial campaign running before the event. I'll be managing the wallet, but in the future, I'll have meme charities control the funds.

Why raise 40k?

The K9 Dog Walk is hoping to raise 40k for dog cancer by January 26th. They are already half way there. If we can get to 20k, that would be awesome, but let's shoot for the moon! Donation period ends January 27th at 11:59pm.

K9 Cancer Walk

Cancer is a disease that touches many of us, that is why Morris Animal Foundation is dedicated to finding a cure. An estimated 50 percent of all dogs will battle cancer. It is the No. 1 cause of death in dogs over the age of 2.

All dog lovers are invited to walk with their dogs to celebrate the life of their canine best friend or to walk in memory of dogs that have lost their battle with canine cancer. We will have an expo area of dog related vendors and a celebration after the walk. Sign up today and help create a healthier tomorrow for dogs everywhere!
